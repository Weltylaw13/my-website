Practice Areas

Welty Legal, PC represents individuals and businesses across the following areas of practice.

Real Estate

Counsel on residential and commercial real estate transactions, including purchase and sale agreements, leases, title and escrow issues, and property disputes.

Estates and Trusts

Estate planning, wills, trusts, trust administration, and probate proceedings, helping clients protect and transfer assets according to their wishes.

Business Matters

Entity formation, governance, contracts, and general counsel support for businesses of all sizes.

Insurance Recovery

Representing policyholders in disputes with insurers, including coverage analysis, claims disputes, and recovery litigation.

Advising Services for Beverage Transactions

Transactional and regulatory advising for wineries, breweries, distilleries, and other beverage industry businesses, including agreements specific to production, distribution, and sale.
